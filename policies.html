Tournament Policies

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our tournament platform.

Information We Collect: We collect information necessary for tournament registration and participation, including your game ID, contact information, and payment details for entry fees.

How We Use Your Information:

  • To register you for tournaments and manage your participation
  • To communicate important updates about tournaments
  • To process payments and distribute rewards
  • To improve our services and user experience

Data Protection: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Contact our support team for assistance with data-related requests.

Terms of Service

By participating in our tournaments, you agree to abide by the following terms and conditions:

Eligibility: Participants must be at least 13 years old to enter our tournaments. Some tournaments may have additional age restrictions based on game requirements.

Player Conduct:

  • All participants must follow fair play guidelines and exhibit good sportsmanship
  • Cheating, exploiting game bugs, or using unauthorized software will result in immediate disqualification
  • Harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior toward other players is strictly prohibited
  • Players must respect tournament organizers and follow their instructions

Tournament Rules:

  1. Participants must join matches on time according to the tournament schedule
  2. Technical issues must be reported immediately to tournament organizers
  3. Decisions made by tournament administrators are final
  4. We reserve the right to modify tournament rules as needed to ensure fair competition

Account Responsibility: You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account and ensuring that all information provided is accurate and up-to-date.

Refund Policy

Our refund policy is designed to be fair to all participants while maintaining the integrity of our tournaments.

Refund Eligibility: Entry fees are fully refundable if a tournament is canceled by the organizers. Partial refunds may be available in cases of technical issues that prevent participation.

Refund Scenarios:

  • Tournament Cancellation: Full refunds will be processed automatically within 7 business days
  • Player Withdrawal: Refunds for voluntary withdrawal are available if requested at least 24 hours before the tournament start time
  • Technical Issues: Refunds may be granted for verifiable technical problems that prevented participation
  • Disqualification: No refunds will be issued for disqualification due to rule violations

Refund Process: To request a refund, contact our support team with your tournament details and reason for the request. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after approval.

Non-Refundable Items: Service fees, processing charges, and donations to prize pools are non-refundable once a tournament has begun.

Prize Distribution

This policy outlines how tournament prizes are distributed to winners.

Payment Timeline: Prize money is typically distributed within 14 business days after tournament completion, pending verification of results.

Distribution Methods: Prizes may be distributed via various methods including direct bank transfer, digital wallets, or in-game currency, depending on the tournament and winner's preference.

Verification Process: All winners must complete identity verification before receiving prizes. This may include providing valid identification and completing tax forms for larger prizes.

Tax Responsibility: Winners are solely responsible for any taxes or fees associated with their prizes. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for guidance.

Disputes: Any disputes regarding prize distribution must be submitted in writing within 30 days of tournament completion.

Code of Conduct

All participants in FreeFire Tournaments are expected to adhere to this code of conduct to ensure a positive gaming environment for everyone.

Respectful Behavior: Treat all players, organizers, and spectators with respect. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

Prohibited Actions:

  • Using cheats, hacks, or any unauthorized third-party software
  • Smurfing (using alternate accounts to gain unfair advantage)
  • Stream sniping or ghosting
  • Collusion or match fixing
  • Impersonating other players or staff members

Communication Guidelines:

  1. Keep all communication respectful and appropriate for all ages
  2. Do not share personal information of other participants
  3. Follow specific channel rules in Whatsapp or other communication platforms
  4. Report any violations to tournament administrators

Consequences: Violations of this code may result in warnings, temporary bans, permanent bans from future tournaments, or forfeiture of prizes, depending on the severity of the offense.
